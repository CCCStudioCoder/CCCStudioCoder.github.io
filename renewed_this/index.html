    • Welcome to Renewed this mod wiki

    Welcome to the “Renewed this” mod wiki. It is currently being created, but is still available.

    The “Renewed this” mod aims to make all Minecraft objects and blocks renewable. Not unlike many mods (or datapacks), this one doesn't just add recipes, but also, and above all, worked gamplay elements such as new ores or special tools.
