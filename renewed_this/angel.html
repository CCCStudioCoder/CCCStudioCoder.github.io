    • The Angel is a rare, strange creature that spawns in the End and attacks the player, endermites and shulkers with its breath.

    Behavior

    The angel always seeks out and attacks players first, then shulkers, then endermites. It has a speed factor equal to 0.5 and the height of its steps is equal to 0.8 blocks without jumping, even though it can fly (for more details on attributes, click here!). It can follow the player up to 32 blocks away and track him up to 128 blocks.
    His “angel's breath” attack is activated every 30 ticks (1.5 seconds) and has a range of 8 blocks. It inflicts 5 damage (2.5 hearts) and a 2-block knockback. The angel has 50 HP (25 hearts), 3 armor points (equivalent to diamond boots or one and a half armor symbols) and no knockback resistance. It drops elytra and 20 experience points when killed. Note that he is immune to :

    • Lightning (even if there is none in the End)
    • Potion effects
    • Fall damage (even if flying)
    • Explosions
    • Drowning
    • Anvil falls

    Spawn

    The angel spawns in all End biomes (all those contained in tag minecraft:is_end ). It is quite rare in terms of loot: it has a spawn weight of 8. It spawns according to the “monster” category in groups of 1 or 2. It can dispawn if inactive.
